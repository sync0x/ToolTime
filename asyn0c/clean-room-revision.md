# Clean-Room Revision Intention

## Status
Intent declaration only. No automation, rewriting pipeline, or alternate-history publishing is being implemented at this time.

## Purpose
We will maintain two distinct public narratives in the future:

- **sync0x/ToolTime**: experimental, transparent, includes missteps and iterative learning.
- **sync3x/ToolTime**: curated, training-friendly, higher-confidence publication stream.

## Future Direction (Deferred)
At a later stage—when this fork has diverged substantially—we may introduce a reproducible process that derives a cleaner instructional history from the experimental line.

Possible attributes of that future process:

1. deterministic regeneration from selected checkpoints,
2. simplified commit grouping/message normalization,
3. optional timeline shaping for readability,
4. explicit provenance and attribution preservation,
5. release gating with stronger testing/audit criteria before publication to `sync3x`.

## Guardrails
Until that future work begins:

- keep `sync0x` as the primary development record,
- avoid manual history rewriting for curated output,
- document decisions so migration to a clean-room publication workflow can be audited later.

## Note
This file intentionally records **intent only** and serves as a placeholder for future policy and tooling.
